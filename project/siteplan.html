Personal Travel Blog Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Wanderlust Chronicles

Reason: This name captures the essence of a passionate traveler documenting their adventures and experiences.

Optional Domain: wanderlustchronicles.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a platform to share travel experiences, tips, and stories. It aims to inspire and assist fellow travelers by providing practical advice and showcasing beautiful destinations.

Scenarios

  • What are some budget-friendly travel tips?
  • How can I plan a trip to [specific destination]?

Color Schema

Background Color: #3498db (used for the main background)

Accent Color: #e74c3c (used for headings and important accents)

Typography

Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for headings)

Body Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text)
